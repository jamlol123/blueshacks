Lesson 2: Interacting with a Friend

Interacting with friends is usually informal and relaxed. Here are some tips for effective communication:

  • Be yourself and be honest.
  • Use casual language and be open to jokes and humor.
  • Listen actively and share your thoughts and feelings.

Remember, friendships are built on trust and genuine connections.
