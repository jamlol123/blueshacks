Lesson 3: Interacting with a Professional

Interacting with professionals, such as doctors or lawyers, requires a respectful and formal approach. Here are some tips for effective communication:

  • Address them with their appropriate title (e.g., Dr., Esq.).
  • Use formal and respectful language.
  • Ask questions relevant to their expertise.

Remember, professionals appreciate professionalism and respect in their interactions.
