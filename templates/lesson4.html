Lesson 4: Interacting with Strangers

Interacting with strangers can be a common occurrence in various situations. Here are some tips for effective communication:

  • Start with a polite greeting.
  • Be open and approachable.
  • Use active listening skills.
  • Respect personal boundaries.

Remember, positive interactions with strangers can lead to meaningful connections.
