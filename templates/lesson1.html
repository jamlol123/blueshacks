Lesson 1: Interacting with a Teacher

Interacting with a teacher or instructor requires a respectful and attentive approach. Here are some tips for effective communication:

  • Address them with their appropriate title (e.g., Mr., Ms., Professor).
  • Be polite and use formal language, especially in educational settings.
  • Ask questions when you need clarification on a topic.
  • Listen actively and engage in discussions related to the subject.

Remember, teachers appreciate students who are eager to learn and show respect in their interactions.
